Rev Rooms

Rev Rooms is a co-development game audio studio, working alongside you at every stage of production to handle all your game's audio needs.

The studio

The well-equipped and snappily-decorated studios are in Brighton, on the South Coast of England. Featuring eight studio rooms, with facilities for recording and mixing both 5.1 + 7.1. Each studio room is lockable, and can be provided with its own IP address.

Developing the Audio & Tools

As a Senior Audio Programmer at Rev Rooms, I am working fully remote with a team of sound designers to develop and maintain audio systems, pipelines and tools for Rev Room's AAA projects.

Stack:

Unreal Engine 5.2 | C++ | Python | Wwise

The team at Rev Rooms have countless years of experience in games and film, bringing creativity and passion to your projects.
