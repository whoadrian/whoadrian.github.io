Bossgard

Being the big bad guy feels great! A 1v5 asymmetrical multiplayer game, ONE BIG PLAYABLE BOSS against a colorful cast of cartoony Vikings.

Bossgard is a wildly colourful game of vikings and weird bosses. Each of the 6 total character can be played or simulated by an AI, including the boss!

The game can be played both online and local multiplayer, and is available on Steam, PS4 and XBox!
For more of its features and mechanics, check out the official website.
