Professional Work

SEED MMO

Audio, Gameplay & Tools Coder @ Klang Games, Berlin

Seed is a persistent MMO simulation where the player's goal is to take over a vast planet, help rebuild civilisation and ensure the survival of humanity.

PROJECT BLACK

Audio & Tools Coder @ Fishlabs, Hamburg

Project Black is a 3rd-person fantasy action game in a lush alient world with a mind of its own, dangerous creatures and hidden treasures.

BOSSGARD

Gameplay & Rendering Coder @ Sand Sailor, Bucharest

Bossgard is a fast-paced asymmetrical multiplayer game, networked or shared-screen, in which Vikings fight a variety of unique playable bosses.

BLACK THE FALL

Rendering & Console Coder @ Sand Sailor, Bucharest

VELAWOODS
